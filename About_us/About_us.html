About Us

ABOUT US

Rawalpindi Flying Club is recognized as the oldest and the most trusted Aviation Training Institutes in Pakistan. Founded in 1957 and since then has produced the finest pilots in the country.
Most of these pilots are working in national airlines, charter companies, and abroad. Moreover, the club has a history of employing prominent flying instructors, having thousands of hours of experience. The big names of Pakistan Air Force like Air Commodore Zafar Masud and A.W. Mufti were part of this club flight instructor team.

Our experienced training squad will give you the training, you require to successfully qualify as an airline pilot. Come and Join us!

100% maintained fleet available

The club owns one of the most experienced engineers, who have extensive experience in PIA and PAF. Safety of the aircraft and pilots is their top-most priority. Their commitment to work can be seen by the fact that the club has 100 % safety record.

Interactive Training Experience

The club pays utmost attention to each individual student. Each student is briefed throughout various stages of training. The shortcomings are taken into consideration and instructors attempts to facilitate each student to overcome them.

Future Growth Plans

Keeping in view the demand of pilots required by the aviation industry worldwide, the club has future plans to expand it’s fleet. The club plans to own two more Cessna aircrafts (C-152/ C-172). This will allow training of more pilots on a faster pace.

Fully accredited and secure

We have been accredited with Pakistan Centre for Philanthropy (PCP). This fact proves that you'll be at the academy that supplies the airlines with the best pilots. As a fully accredited flight academy, we have built our name an reputation for over 60 years.
