We are RFC, a first-class commercial pilot training academy founded in 1957 situated near Benazir Bhutto International Airport, Islamabad, Pakistan. RFC is regarded as the oldest and finest pilot producing clubs nationwide. Most of our trained pilots are working in National Airlines, Charter Companies and Abroad. We help our students improve the skills, confidence & experience to attain the hallmark of commercial airline pilots.

RFC students loves to be a part of close knit community and train close by others that share the same dream: to emerge as a pilot.
